# 🏡 Home

# 🌄 Why

Content to teach the world about software, programming, and engineering topics of interest - to engage with excellence and the distribution of great ideas.

# 🌋 How

To help put together this knowledge; the software-engineering-at-home organisation (opens new window), has been created as a home for content - such that topics about software, programming, and engineering can be brought together and made available through GitHub, Twitter, and YouTube.

# 🏔️ What

This website; the Github repository, the YouTube channel, the Twitter page. The content, the users, the feedback you give me!
